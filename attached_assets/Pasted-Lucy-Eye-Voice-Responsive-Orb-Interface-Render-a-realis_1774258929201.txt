INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Background & Eye Placement:
- Use the existing eye background image.
- Center the eye both vertically and horizontally on the page.
- The black pupil should be **aligned at the exact center of the screen**.
- The eye should remain static (no full 3D movement) to save GPU.

2. Orb / Ring Placement:
- Overlay a circle (orb or ring) directly on top of the pupil.
- Orb dimensions: roughly 20–30% of pupil size (adjust visually).
- Center orb on pupil coordinates.
- Orb color states:
   - Dormant: black / very dark gray (#1a1a1a)
   - Listening: soft Apple-blue (#0A84FF)
   - Thinking: deeper blue (#0051D5)
   - Speaking: bright blue with subtle glow

3. Orb Animation:
- Voice amplitude drives orb scale / glow intensity.
- Listening: slow subtle pulse.
- Thinking: slow rotation or subtle ring expansion.
- Speaking: scale up slightly, optional micro glow rings.
- Keep all animations lightweight (SVG or Canvas preferred) to avoid Safari crashes.

4. Continuous Interaction:
- Orb reacts in real-time without requiring clicks.
- Barge-in: user can speak at any time; orb immediately switches state to listening and audio stream resumes.
- Maintain persistent AudioContext and TTS streaming.

5. Frontend Considerations:
- Use React.memo or similar to avoid re-rendering the full orb for every transcript update.
- Throttle DOM/CSS animations to max 60ms intervals.
- Ensure eye + orb layout scales responsively for desktop and mobile screens.
- Optional: micro-blinks of the eye every few seconds for personality, very subtle to avoid CPU load.

RESULT:
- Eye centered on screen with pupil as focal point.
- Interactive Apple-blue orb/ring overlayed on pupil.
- Orb reacts fluidly to voice in real-time.
