Lucy — Continuous Voice, 3D Eye + Orb, Crash-Free Optimization

OBJECTIVE:
- Make Lucy fully interactive with fluent, multi-turn voice conversation.
- Eye + orb appear as a living interface: realistic pupil + interactive Apple-blue orb/ring overlay.
- Ensure stable performance on desktop, Safari, and low-spec machines.

---

1. Continuous Voice Interaction:
- After wake-word “Lucy”, enter an **always-on listening session** until explicit stop or session timeout (45–60s).
- Microphone remains open; do not require clicking the orb for follow-ups.
- Implement **barge-in**: if the user speaks while Lucy is speaking, immediately stop TTS and switch orb to listening.
- Maintain last 8–12 conversation turns in memory for context-aware responses.
- Use streaming STT with partial transcript handling for low-latency response (<300ms).

2. Orb / Eye Interface:
- Replace static pupil `<img>` with a **lightweight 3D render** (Three.js / WebGL) if possible, or Canvas/SVG overlay for performance.
- Eye is static; centered vertically & horizontally on the screen.
- Pupil coordinates aligned to screen center.
- Overlay a small interactive orb/ring on the pupil:
  - Dormant: black (#1a1a1a) with subtle specular highlight.
  - Listening: soft Apple-blue (#0A84FF)
  - Thinking: deeper blue (#0051D5) with breathing pulse
  - Speaking: bright blue with glow/ring animation
- Voice amplitude drives orb/ring scale and glow.
- Optional: subtle eye micro-blinks or pupil dilation every few seconds for realism, CPU-friendly.

3. Session Handling & Performance:
- Persistent AudioContext; mic opened once per session.
- Stop individual buffer sources instead of closing full context.
- Throttle STT input & orb updates to max 60ms intervals.
- Partial audio streaming for TTS; free memory buffers immediately after use.
- Session timeout 45–60s; resets on every interaction.
- Wake-word listener re-armed automatically after timeout.

4. UX & Micro-Interactions:
- Thinking micro-fillers while processing: cycle “Hmm… / Got it… / Right… / Let me think… / Sure…” every 1.8s.
- Orb visually reacts to voice states (listening, thinking, speaking) continuously.
- Task board / optional dashboard can appear below logo for productivity tasks, fully voice-integrated.

5. Testing / Browser Considerations:
- Must be Safari-friendly; low CPU/GPU usage.
- Ensure multi-turn conversation continues fluidly in various environments (office, car, street).
- Validate continuous voice without needing clicks after wake-word.
- Ensure the orb always aligns with the pupil and responds smoothly.

---

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
- Eye centered on screen with pupil as focal point.
- Orb/ring reacts live to voice without clicks.
- Full continuous conversation with context memory.
- Lightweight, crash-free performance across desktop, mobile, and Safari.
